About Me

I am a full stack web developer, in training, and I am passionate about embedded software development for the Internet of Things. Like most developers, I earned my foundational skills by obsessively tinkering with microcontrollers for hours in my garage. My passion for IoT full stack development is uniquely rooted in my long term goal of one day launching a biotech company with a focus on crop yield and irrigation efficiency.

In order to springboard my skills beyond the level of a DIY hacker, I enrolled in the Trilogy Education Services Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp, located at the University of Central Florida.
